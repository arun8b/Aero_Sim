Portfolio roadmap

Open-Source Simulation Development Plan

  1. Build the GitHub Pages site as a central portfolio hub, linking each project to its methodology, results and engineering interpretation.
  2. Complete the OpenFOAM and SU2 projects first, as these demonstrate the most directly relevant CFD capability for aerospace, aerodynamics and flight-physics roles.
  3. Add the FEniCSx structural panel project to show finite-element modelling capability and broader continuum-mechanics understanding.
  4. Develop the PyFR, FiPy and Dedalus projects as advanced numerical-methods showcases, covering high-order CFD, finite-volume transport and spectral-method analysis.
